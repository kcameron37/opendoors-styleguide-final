Colours and Use

Overall, Open Doors was looking for a warm and inviting clours scheme. One that allowed individuals to feel calm and stability when they used the app

#FF9D60

This color is a bright, vibrant orange. It has a warm and inviting feel to it and this will be an accent colour for the app

#FFB374

This color is a soft, peach with a warm undertone. It will be used to create a calming and relaxing mood. This will be the primary colour for the app

#33718A

This color is a deep, rich blue-green. It has a cool and calming feel to it, and could be used to create a sense of stability and reliability. This will be the secondary colour for the app.

#94C3CD

This color is a light, blue-green with a cool undertone. It is Open Door's secondary colour and it will be used to create a sense of tranquility and calmness. This will be an accent colour for the app

#EAD9C5

This color is a soft and warm beige. It has a cozy and comforting feel to it, and could be used to create a sense of warmth and familiarity. It will be the background colour for the app
