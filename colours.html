Colours and Use

Overall, Open Doors was looking for a warm and inviting colour scheme. One that allowed individuals to feel calm and stability when they used the app

#FF9D60

This color is a bright, vibrant orange. It has a warm and inviting feel to it and this will be an accent colour for the app

#FFB374

This color is a soft, peach with a warm undertone. It will be used to create a calming and relaxing mood. This will be the primary colour for the app and will be used for the header, footer and buttons

#33718A

This color is a deep, rich blue-green. It has a cool and calming feel to it, and could be used to create a sense of stability and reliability. This will be the secondary colour for the app, including on buttons

#94C3CD

This color is a light, blue-green with a cool undertone. It is Open Door's secondary colour and it will be used to create a sense of tranquility and calmness. This will be an accent colour for the app

#FFFFFF

White adds a clear and simple look to the page. It will be the background colour for the app



Typography

Overall, Open Doors wants to have easy to read text but also a display text that stood out from the rest

Header 1

Calistoga, Size 40

This is Open Doors H1 font. We chose it because is has a friendly and welcoming and it is unique display

Header 2

Calistoga, Size 30, Light

This is Open Doors H2 font. This will be our primary subheader

Header 3

Calistoga, Size 20, Light

This is Open Doors H3 font. It will be our subheader on darker backgrounds

Header 4

Archivo Narrow, Size 30

This will be our H4. This font is easy to read and will act our main text on our quiz pages

Header 5

Roboto, Sixe 20

This text will be our primaary font for buttoms and the navigation bar

Header 6

Roboto, Size 20, Light

This is Open Door H6 font. It will be used in our footer

Paragraph

Roboto, Size 14

This is our paragraph text. It is easy to read and will be for any body text within the app

Link

Roboto, Size 14

Any links in the Open Doors app will have a underline

Hover Link

Roboto, Size 14

When hovering the link will turn white on links that are on coloured backgrounds and blue on the main white background
